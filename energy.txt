Energy:
- Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed.
- Types: Kinetic, potential, thermal, chemical, electrical, nuclear.
- Renewable energy: solar, wind, hydro, geothermal.
- Fossil fuels: coal, oil, natural gas.

Units of Energy:
- Joule (J), calorie, kilowatt-hour (kWh).

Energy Sources:
- Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity.
- Wind turbines use air flow to generate power.
- Hydroelectric dams generate power from water flow.

Fossil Fuels:
- Burning coal releases carbon dioxide.
- Oil is refined into gasoline and diesel.
- Natural gas is used in heating and electricity.

Energy Innovations:
- Smart grids manage electricity distribution.
- Electric vehicles reduce dependence on fossil fuels.
