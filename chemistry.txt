Chemistry:
- Study of matter and its interactions.

Atoms and Elements:
- Atom: Smallest unit of matter.
- Elements: Pure substances (e.g., H, O, C).

States of Matter:
- Solid, liquid, gas, plasma.

Chemical Reactions:
- Combustion, oxidation, acid-base, precipitation.
- Law of Conservation of Mass: Matter is neither created nor destroyed.

Periodic Table:
- Organized by atomic number and properties.
