Continents Overview:

Africa:
- Second-largest continent.
- Home to Sahara Desert, Nile River.

Asia:
- Largest continent.
- Includes China, India, Japan.

Europe:
- Rich history and culture.
- Includes Germany, France, UK.

North America:
- Includes USA, Canada, Mexico.

South America:
- Home to Amazon Rainforest.

Australia:
- Smallest continent, diverse wildlife.

Antarctica:
- Coldest, covered in ice, no permanent population.
