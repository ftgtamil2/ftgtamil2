Machines:
- Simple machines: lever, wheel and axle, pulley, inclined plane, wedge, screw.
- Complex machines: Combine multiple simple machines.
- Engines convert fuel into motion.
- Robots use sensors and software to perform tasks.

Examples:
- Cars, airplanes, washing machines, computers.

Types of Machines:
- Mechanical: Engines, pulleys, gears.
- Electrical: Motors, generators, transformers.
- Digital: Computers, smartphones, smart devices.

Engineering Fields:
- Mechanical: Design and analysis of machines.
- Electrical: Power systems and electronics.
- Robotics: Integration of software, mechanics, electronics.

Examples of Innovations:
- Autonomous vehicles.
- AI-powered robots.
- 3D printers.
