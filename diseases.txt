Diseases:
- Diabetes: High blood sugar levels.
- Hypertension: High blood pressure.
- Cancer: Uncontrolled cell growth.
- Asthma: Inflammation of airways.

Disease Prevention:
- Healthy diet, regular exercise, vaccinations, hygiene.

Types of Diseases:
- Infectious: Caused by pathogens (bacteria, viruses, fungi).
- Genetic: Inherited from parents (e.g., cystic fibrosis).
- Lifestyle: Linked to habits (e.g., obesity, heart disease).

Treatment Methods:
- Antibiotics for bacterial infections.
- Vaccines for prevention.
- Surgery, therapy, and medication for various conditions.

Health Tips:
- Drink clean water.
- Maintain hygiene.
- Regular medical checkups.
