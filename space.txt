Space Facts:
- The Sun is a star at the center of our solar system.
- Earth is the third planet from the Sun.
- A light-year is about 9.46 trillion kilometers.
- The Moon affects Earth's tides.

Planets:
- Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune.

Astronomy:
- There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on Earth.
- The Andromeda Galaxy is on a collision course with the Milky Way.
- Neutron stars are so dense that a teaspoon would weigh billions of tons.

Astronomical Phenomena:
- Solar eclipses occur when the Moon blocks the Sun.
- A supernova is the explosive death of a star.
- Black holes can bend light with gravity.

Space Missions:
- Apollo 11 landed humans on the Moon in 1969.
- The James Webb Space Telescope studies the early universe.
