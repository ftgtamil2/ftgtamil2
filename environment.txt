Environment:
- Climate change is caused by excess greenhouse gases.
- Deforestation reduces biodiversity.
- Recycling reduces waste in landfills.
- Renewable energy sources include solar, wind, and hydro.

Pollution Types:
- Air pollution, water pollution, soil pollution, noise pollution.

Ecosystems:
- Forests, oceans, deserts, wetlands are all types of ecosystems.
- Biodiversity is essential for ecosystem stability.
- Coral reefs are among the most diverse ecosystems.

Environmental Protection:
- Planting trees helps combat climate change.
- Conservation efforts aim to protect endangered species.
- Reducing plastic use cuts down ocean pollution.

Sustainable Living:
- Use of eco-friendly products.
- Reducing carbon footprint by biking or using public transport.
