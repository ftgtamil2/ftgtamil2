Health:
- State of physical, mental, and social well-being.

Nutrition:
- Carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals.
- Balanced diet promotes good health.

Fitness:
- Physical activity improves cardiovascular health and strength.
- Recommended: 30 minutes of exercise daily.

Mental Health:
- Includes emotional and psychological well-being.
- Stress management and social support are important.
