Artificial Intelligence (AI):
- Simulation of human intelligence in machines.

Types of AI:
- Narrow AI: Specialized tasks (e.g., chatbots, recommendation systems).
- General AI: Human-like thinking (theoretical).
- Superintelligent AI: Beyond human capabilities (hypothetical).

Applications:
- Healthcare: Diagnosis, robot surgery.
- Transport: Self-driving cars.
- Finance: Fraud detection, trading algorithms.

Machine Learning:
- Subfield of AI: systems learn from data.
