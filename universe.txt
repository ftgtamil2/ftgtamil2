Universe:
- Estimated to be 13.8 billion years old.
- Composed of galaxies, stars, planets, black holes.
- Milky Way is our galaxy.
- Dark matter and dark energy make up most of the universe.

Cosmic Events:
- Supernovae, black holes, cosmic microwave background.

Galaxies:
- Spiral, elliptical, and irregular are the main types.
- The Milky Way contains over 100 billion stars.
- Galaxies form clusters and superclusters.

Cosmology:
- The Big Bang Theory explains the origin of the universe.
- The universe is expanding.
- Dark energy accelerates the expansion.

Exploration Tools:
- Telescopes collect light to observe distant objects.
- Probes send data from space missions.
